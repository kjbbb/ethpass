Use CryptoPass

cryptopass

CryptoPass is a decentralized application (DAPP) to store your passwords securely on the blockchain, powered by Ethereum. Your encrypted data is stored redundantly across the network, so that they are never lost, and are only accessible by you.

Blockchain Level Security 

Open Source

Our contract is open source for everyone to see and audit. It is only 20 lines long.

Available Everywhere

Your passwords are stored on tens of thousands of machines, on one of the largest distributed supercomputers on the planet, available anywhere that has internet. CryptoPass can even store your passwords encrypted offline in a 'hard wallet,' and don't need to be synced to the blockchain.

You're In Charge

Your passwords are encrypted and decrypted client-side. They are never sent in plain-text across a network. Only the ethereum wallet key-holder can encrypt and decrypt them.

Secure

We leverage the blockchain's built-in security to handle encrypting and storing your passwords. Blockchains are trusted with money, and that trust can be extended to passwords.

No signup

We do not ask for your email. You only need an ethereum wallet, which can even be created offline.

Free To Retrieve

Your passwords are free to retrieve from the network. Your data can't be held hostage, as long as a computer running ethereum still exists. If we go down, your passwords will live and be accessible. You need a small amount of ether (10 cents worth) to sync them to the network, due to the miner fee, but storing them offline is free.
